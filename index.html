Pandemic


There is a global pandemic! Everyone is infected! Fight to survive or find a cure!

2-4 players play cards to either find a cure or sabotage others.


Increase your research to find a cure, and decrease your infection to stay alive.


Decrease others research while increasing their infection to try and kill them faster.


Each turn is 20 seconds and after 20 seconds everyone's infection increases by their current infection rate.


If you play a card your turn is over, but the infection timer is not affected.


To win you must get 100 or more research, or be the last one alive.


If you reach 100 infection or more, you will die.


Cards

  • Increase Research:
    • Increase your current research by 20.
  • Increase Infection:
    • Increase your target's infection by 10.
  • Decrease Infection:
    • Decrease your current infection by 10.
  • Decrease Research:
    • Decrease your target's research by 10.
  • Infection Strength Increase:
    • Increase your target's rate of infection per turn by 10.
  • Infection Strength Decrease:
    • Decrease your rate of infection per turn by 10.
  • Reset Infection Rate:
    • Reset rate of infection on target back to 10.
  • Temporary Cure:
    • Reset your total infection to 0.
